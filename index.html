About & History

Our Origin Story

Xtra Manager Software was officially founded on January 16, 2025 by Gusti Aditya Muzaky.

What started as a personal passion project quickly evolved into a collaborative open-source community dedicated to pushing open source programs development boundaries.

Our Mission

We exist to create a thriving ecosystem where developers, power users, enthusiasts on open source can collaborate on tools that enhance programs experience. Our focus areas include:

  • Kernel optimization & management
  • System diagnostics & analysis
  • Linux integration on Android
  • Network management utilities
  • ROM development toolkits
